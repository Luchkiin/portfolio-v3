About me?

Lucas Rosendahl

Stockholm-based designer with a genuine interest in UX & UI design and front-end development.

My expertise is based on a bachelor's degree in systems science but also stems from different skills I have picked up in the field of design and IT. These skills include developing techniques and design principles that interest and define me as a designer.

Things I excel at are UX & UI design, where I have an eye for detail in what I create. With my zealous way of working, I possess a mindset for design and usability where I always strive to create user-friendly and memorable products for the end-user to experience.

This portfolio includes a wide range of works that I’ve either done during my university years, as an in-house/external employee, or personal projects in my free time. I hope you like it and feel free to contact me using the provided links below.
